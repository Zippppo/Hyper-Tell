Measurement scope. Shapes are read from voxel_labels.npy header inside each .npz. This measures native stored voxel grid extents used as the shared grid for labels and sensor-point occupancy. It does not measure a tight bounding box of only nonzero label voxels, only body-surface points, or the union of labels and body surface.

Training semantics: Body-Tell voxelizes sensor_pc into voxel_labels.shape, then applies the same volume_size crop/pad to voxel_labels and occupancy.

The axis-quantile boxes answer "what D/H/W independently covers p% of each axis". The joint-optimized boxes answer "what minimum-volume D/H/W covers p% of complete cases". Use the joint-optimized rows when the objective is the most economical fixed volume_size.
