Task: Support aggregate and combined-label concepts in sampler.

Recommendation: ready_for_review

Summary

The training prompt sampler now uses a concept index instead of a class-only index. Base concepts still target one class; aggregate concepts stay marked with source_type="aggregate" and target the union of their configured component class ids. Aggregate presence is recomputed from patch-local component voxel counts, so positive aggregate masks use the component union and negative aggregate targets remain empty.
